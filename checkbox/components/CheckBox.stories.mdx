# Check Box
___
`Checkbox` will serve as generic checkbox for kaios app.

## Usage

> Props that can be passed are:

<Props of={Checkbox}/>

Two colors cane be passed(blue | white) and Checkbox can have four states-
___
1. Unchecked
1. Checked
1. Uncheck Disabled
1. Checked Disabled
